Who We Are

About Ash Hu Music

A YouTube-focused label with complete artist development services

Ash Hu Music is an independent music label founded by young entrepreneurs Harshit Rao and Udghosh Rao. While our primary focus is YouTube growth and content strategy, we offer comprehensive services including distribution, branding, marketing, and artist development. We believe in growing artists organically through smart content planning, professional branding, and strategic releases. Our approach combines modern digital strategies with traditional music industry expertise to help independent artists build sustainable careers.

Harshit Rao

Founder & CEO

Harshit Rao is the Founder & CEO of Ash Hu Music, bringing fresh perspectives to the music industry. He oversees label operations, artist partnerships, YouTube strategy, and release planning. Harshit's vision is to create a modern label that prioritizes artist growth and sustainable development through smart digital strategies and organic audience building.

Udghosh Rao

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Udghosh Rao leads all creative aspects at Ash Hu Music as Co-Founder & Creative Director. He specializes in visual branding, thumbnail design, cover art, and artist identity development. Udghosh ensures every artist looks professional and stands out on YouTube and social media platforms through compelling visuals and strategic creative direction.
