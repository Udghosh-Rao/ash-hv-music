Who We Are

About Ash Hu Music

Independent music label dedicated to artist success

Ash Hu Music is an independent music label founded by young entrepreneurs Harshit Rao and Udghosh Rao. We provide comprehensive services for independent artists including global distribution, professional branding, strategic marketing, and platform growth. Our approach combines data-driven strategies with creative excellence to help artists reach their full potential across streaming platforms, social media, and beyond.

Harshit Rao

Founder & CEO

Harshit Rao is the Founder & CEO of Ash Hu Music. He oversees label operations, artist partnerships, release planning, and business development. Harshit's vision is to create a modern label that prioritizes artist growth through smart strategies, organic audience building, and sustainable career development.

Udghosh Rao

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Udghosh Rao leads all creative aspects at Ash Hu Music as Co-Founder & Creative Director. He specializes in visual branding, cover art, promotional design, and artist identity development. Udghosh ensures every artist presents professionally across all platforms through compelling visuals and strategic creative direction.
