Who We Are

A new music label built by young entrepreneurs, for artists, with passion and innovation at our core

Ash Hu Music is a brand new independent music label founded by two passionate young entrepreneurs, Harshit Rao and Udghosh Rao, who share a vision of empowering independent artists in the digital age. We believe that every artist deserves access to professional-grade distribution, marketing, and development services without sacrificing creative control or fair compensation.

As a new label, we're hungry to prove ourselves and dedicated to building strong relationships with artists. Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with personalized support to help musicians navigate the complex music industry landscape. From global distribution across 200+ platforms to strategic playlist pitching, professional branding, and comprehensive marketing solutions, we provide everything artists need to build sustainable careers.

Meet The Founders

Two young friends with a shared vision of revolutionizing independent music distribution

Harshit Rao

Founder & CEO

Co-Founder & Strategic Leader

Harshit Rao is the Founder & CEO of Ash Hu Music, bringing fresh energy and entrepreneurial vision to the independent music industry. He oversees label operations, artist partnerships, release planning, and business development. Despite being new to the industry, Harshit's passion for music and dedication to artist success drives everything we do. His goal is to create a modern label that prioritizes artist growth through smart strategies, organic audience building, and sustainable career development.

Udghosh Rao

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Co-Founder & Visual Innovator

Udghosh Rao leads all creative aspects at Ash Hu Music as Co-Founder & Creative Director. He specializes in visual branding, cover art design, promotional graphics, and artist identity development. As a young creative entrepreneur, Udghosh brings a fresh perspective to artist presentation and ensures every artist looks professional across all platforms through compelling visuals and strategic creative direction. What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions designed to elevate every aspect of your music career

Global Distribution

Get your music on 200+ platforms worldwide including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, Deezer, and all major streaming services with lightning-fast delivery and reliable support.

Brand Development

Build a compelling artist identity with professional branding, logo design, visual content creation, and cohesive aesthetic that captures audience attention and builds lasting recognition in the industry.

Marketing Strategy

Strategic multi-channel campaigns, social media management, playlist pitching, press coverage, influencer partnerships, and targeted advertising to maximize your reach and fan engagement.

Artist Development

Personalized mentorship, career coaching, skill workshops, performance training, and industry networking opportunities to accelerate your professional growth and long-term success in music.

Analytics & Insights

Advanced performance tracking, real-time data visualization, audience demographics, streaming analytics, and AI-powered recommendations for intelligent, data-driven decision-making.

Sync Licensing

Premium opportunities for music placement in films, TV shows, commercials, video games, and digital content with expert negotiation and professional licensing management support.

Audio Mastering

Professional mastering services with industry-standard equipment, experienced engineers, format optimization, and quality enhancement for all platforms and listening devices worldwide.

Rights Management

Comprehensive copyright protection, worldwide royalty collection, content ID management, dispute resolution, and transparent reporting to ensure you receive every penny you've earned.

Video Production

End-to-end music video production including concept development, professional filming, creative editing, color grading, special effects, and multi-platform distribution optimization.
