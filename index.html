FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and how we help artists succeed

How quickly can my music be distributed?

Your music can be live on all major platforms within 24-48 hours! We offer one of the fastest distribution services in the industry. For urgent releases, we provide same-day distribution for an additional fee. Our streamlined process ensures your music reaches fans quickly without compromising quality.

Do I keep 100% of my royalties?

Absolutely! You keep 100% of your royalties with all our plans. We believe artists deserve to be fairly compensated for their work. Unlike traditional labels that take large percentages, we only charge a simple monthly subscription fee. All streaming revenue goes directly to you, with transparent reporting and monthly payouts.

What platforms do you distribute to?

We distribute to 200+ platforms worldwide including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, Deezer, Pandora, SoundCloud, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and many more. We also cover regional platforms in Asia, Europe, Latin America, and Africa to maximize your global reach.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard. If you cancel, your music will remain live on all platforms for the duration of your paid period. We believe in earning your business every month through excellent service, not binding contracts.

Do you provide playlist pitching services?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans include expert playlist pitching to Spotify editorial playlists, Apple Music curators, and independent playlist curators with millions of followers. Our team has established relationships with key decision-makers and a proven track record of securing high-profile placements.

How does the artist development program work?

Our artist development program (included in Enterprise plan) offers personalized one-on-one mentorship sessions, career strategy planning, skill workshops covering production/marketing/business, performance coaching, industry networking events, and ongoing support from our founders Harshit and Udghosh. It's designed to accelerate your growth and build a sustainable music career.

What makes Ash Hu Music different from other distributors?

Unlike other distributors, we offer a complete ecosystem for artist success: distribution + marketing + branding + development + analytics, all in one platform. Founded by two friends who are passionate about empowering independent artists, we provide personalized attention, cutting-edge technology, and genuine commitment to your success. We're not just a distributor; we're your long-term career partner.

Do you offer sync licensing opportunities?

Yes! Our sync licensing team actively pitches your music to film studios, TV networks, advertising agencies, video game developers, and content creators. We have partnerships with major production companies and have secured placements in Netflix shows, major commercials, and popular video games. Enterprise members get priority consideration for all opportunities.
