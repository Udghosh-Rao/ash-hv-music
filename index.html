About Us

Who We Are

A music label built by artists, for artists, with passion and innovation at our core

Ash Hu Music was founded by two passionate friends, Harshit and Udghosh, who share a vision of empowering independent artists in the digital age. We believe that every artist deserves access to professional-grade distribution, marketing, and development services without sacrificing creative control or fair compensation.

Our label operates on transparency, innovation, and artist-first principles. We combine cutting-edge technology with personalized support to help musicians navigate the complex music industry landscape. From global distribution across 200+ platforms to strategic playlist pitching, sync licensing opportunities, and comprehensive branding solutions, we provide everything artists need to build sustainable careers.

What sets us apart is our commitment to keeping artists in control of their music while providing enterprise-level services typically reserved for major label artists. We're not just a distributorâ€”we're your long-term career partner, dedicated to your success every step of the way.
What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions designed to elevate every aspect of your music career

Global Distribution

Get your music on 200+ platforms worldwide including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, Deezer, and all major streaming services with lightning-fast delivery.

Brand Development

Build a compelling artist identity with professional branding, logo design, visual content creation, and cohesive aesthetic that captures audience attention and builds recognition.

Marketing Strategy

Strategic multi-channel campaigns, social media management, playlist pitching, press coverage, influencer partnerships, and targeted advertising to maximize your reach and engagement.

Artist Development

Personalized mentorship, career coaching, skill workshops, performance training, and industry networking opportunities to accelerate your professional growth and long-term success.

Analytics & Insights

Advanced performance tracking, real-time data visualization, audience demographics, streaming analytics, and AI-powered recommendations for intelligent decision-making.

Sync Licensing

Premium opportunities for music placement in films, TV shows, commercials, video games, and digital content with expert negotiation and licensing management support.

Audio Mastering

Professional mastering services with industry-standard equipment, experienced engineers, format optimization, and quality enhancement for all platforms and devices.

Rights Management

Comprehensive copyright protection, worldwide royalty collection, content ID management, dispute resolution, and transparent reporting to ensure you receive every penny earned.

Video Production

End-to-end music video production including concept development, professional filming, creative editing, color grading, special effects, and multi-platform distribution optimization.
