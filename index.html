About Ash Hu Music

A YouTube-first label for emerging independent artists

Ash Hu Music is an independent, YouTube-first music label focused on emerging artists. We help artists grow using thumbnails, Shorts, branding, and release planning. We do NOT push paid distribution for small artists — we wait until growth is real.

What We Do for Artists

  • YouTube channel & release strategy
  • Thumbnail, title & branding support
  • YouTube Shorts & discovery planning
  • Growth tracking & analytics
  • Distribution only after real traction

Harshit Rao

Founder & CEO

Building a growth-first music label for independent artists

Harshit Rao is the Founder & CEO of Ash Hu Music. He focuses on building a practical, artist-first label model where growth comes before monetization. Harshit works closely with artists on release planning, YouTube strategy, and long-term development. His approach is simple — help artists grow organically first, then introduce distribution only when it makes financial sense.

Udghosh Rao

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Shaping visuals, branding, and artist identity

Udghosh Rao leads the creative direction at Ash Hu Music. He is responsible for thumbnails, cover art, branding, and visual presentation of artists on YouTube and social platforms. Udghosh helps emerging artists look professional and clickable, focusing on designs that support growth rather than unnecessary hype.
