Our Story

About Ash Hu Music

A vision to revolutionize the music industry, one artist at a time

Ash Hu Music was founded on the belief that exceptional talent deserves exceptional support. We're more than a labelwe're a creative ecosystem where artists thrive, innovate, and connect with audiences worldwide. Our commitment to artistic integrity and commercial success has made us a trusted partner for emerging and established musicians alike.

Harshit Rao

Founder & CEO

Visionary entrepreneur with over 15 years in the music industry. Harshit's passion for discovering raw talent and transforming it into global phenomena has shaped Ash Hu Music's core philosophy. His innovative approach to artist development and strategic partnerships has established the label as an industry leader.

Udghosh Rao

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Award-winning music producer and creative strategist. Udghosh brings unparalleled expertise in production, branding, and artistic direction. His deep understanding of musical trends and audience psychology has driven countless chart-topping releases and transformed artist careers across multiple genres.
